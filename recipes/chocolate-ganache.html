Chocolate Ganache Recipe

Description

Learn how to make a creamy and smooth chocolate ganache for your macaron filling

Ingredients

  • 90g dark chocolate
  • 60g whipping cream
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a microwave proof bowl. Microwave for a minute and let sit for 3-5 minutes before stirring until smooth.
  2. Cover and place in the fridge to cool down and thicken.
  3. Use a piping bag to fill the macarons.
