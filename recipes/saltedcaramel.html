Macaron Recipe

Description

Learn how to make the challenging French Macarons

Ingredients

  • 175g granulated sugar
  • 60g water
  • 1 tsp lemon or vinegar
  • 120g heavy whipping cream
    • <1i>175g butter cut into cubes
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a heavy saucepan, combine sugar, water and vinegar and cook over medium to low heat. Do not stir. Wait until color turns into an amber shade.
  2. Turn off burner. Add butter and whisk. Caution: it will steam and bubble up aggressively.
  3. Once the caramel has calmed down, add the whipping cream and salt. Stir until combined.
  4. Transfer to a bowl to cool down before placing in the fridge to thicken.
  6. Use a piping bag to fill the macarons.
